Pentesting cisco meraki

Trying to open up the router w/o subscription
December 28, 2024 · 1 min · 186 words · Me | Suggest Changes

Cisco Meraki is the enterprise router solution for businesses big and small. I got my hands on a router via a close friend who found and old one in his basement.

Cisco Meraki requires a yearly subscription to work. Since my friend was no longer interested in paying it, I decided to take a crack at getting it to work without a subscription.

The cisco meraki hosts a lighttpd webserver on port 80. This webserver trigger the captive portal which tries to get you to sign in. However you can navigate to the router IP and get the a portal.

The first instinct was looking at the webpage being loaded and looking at javascript files to get all the webserver endpoints.

I saved the page locally and ran it though grep
